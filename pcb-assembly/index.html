Printed Circuit Boards Assembly

High Quality Printed Circuit Boards

With a decade of proven track record in Malaysia, Basset has established a reputation for our expertise in producing high-quality printed circuit boards. Located in Petaling Jaya, our manufacturing facility abides my professional standards and procedures to produce a wide range of made-to-order printed circuit boards.

As of 2001, Basset is ISO 9001:2000 certified — a step towards its commitment to professional manufacturing practices.

Basset has the facilities, the capability and the technical expertise for the full range of PCBs.

Assembly Capabilities

High-Speed Automation:

  • PDT-PS100 & SP400V Screen Printers
  • Samsung SM411 High-Speed Placement Machines
  • Samsung SM481 High Speed and Flexible Placement System
  • Samsung SM421 Flexible Placement Systems
  • Samsung SRF70i82 and Derpan TSK800 Reflow Ovens
  • Desktop Automatic Robotic Soldering Systems

 

Component Packages Including:

  • BGA (ceramic, metal plastic)
  • Micro-BGA
  • 0603 – 14mm (as well as less than 14mm) connector part can be placed
  • SOIC
  • PLCC
  • QFP
  • CSP

 

Fine Pitch Placement:

  • Leaded Minimums:
    • .0065″ (.16mm) width
    • .020″ (.50mm) pitch
  • BGA/CSP Minimums:
    • .010″ (.25mm) diameter
    • .026″ (.65mm) pitch

Board Sizes

  • Single-sided and double-sided
  • Thickness 0.38mm – 4.2mm
  • L50 x W40 mm minimum to L510 x W460 mm maximum

 

Board Shape

  • Rectangular
  • Round
  • Slots
  • Cutouts
  • Complex
  • Irregular

 

Board Type

  • Rigid
  • Flexible
  • Rigid-flexible

 

Solder Type

  • Leaded and lead-free
  • Water soluble solder paste
  • Manual soldering for special parts, e.g. wires and temperature sensitive parts

 

Design file format

  • Gerber RS-274X, 274D, Eagle and AutoCAD’s DXF, DWG
  • BOM (Bill of Materials)
  • Pick and Place file (XYRS)

Quality Assurance

All of our products undergo several stages of testing to ensure that they are in working order. All printed circuit boards go through the following quality assurance procedures:

– Visual inspection
– Automated Optical Inspection (AOI)
– In-circuit tests (ICT)
– Functional tests (test modules need to be supplied)

Testing Procedures

Our expert technicians perform AOI testing on PCBA 150x150 printed circuit board assembly services to ensure that all final products that leave our facilities are in top quality.

Packaging

Finished boards are packed in an antistatic bag with unit-pack or multiple-pack boxes. Rest assured that all boards will reach you safely and in top condition.
